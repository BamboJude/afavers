Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

afavers ("we", "us") operates the website afavers.online and the afavers Job Capture browser extension. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account data: Your email address and hashed password, used to authenticate you.
  • Job data: Job listings you choose to save — title, company, location, salary, URL, and notes. This data is stored on your account and is never shared.
  • Authentication token: The browser extension stores a login token locally in your browser to keep you signed in. It is never transmitted to any third party.

2. Browser Extension

The afavers Job Capture extension reads job listing content on pages you visit only when you actively click the extension icon. It extracts job details (title, company, location, salary) and sends them to the afavers server under your account. No browsing history or page content is collected passively.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not track your browsing activity.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or advertising.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored on servers hosted via Railway and Supabase (PostgreSQL), located in the EU. We take reasonable measures to protect your data.

5. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at hello@afavers.online.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at hello@afavers.online.
